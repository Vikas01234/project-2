ICC Cricket World Cup News

The biggest scoreline in the history of the World Cup qualifiers- and indeed in the history of international cricket- was recorded on 2 april 2011,
when india beat sri lanka in the final of world cup.

This legendary match also memorable for master blaster sachin tendulkar because it was the last world cup for the legend.

And though the defeat earned sri lanka, so inspiring has their subsequent recovery been that it is now the subject of an acclaimed documentary.
